We are a collective building spaces at the intersection of art, activism and academia. We bring people together who dare to stay with the trouble of extradisciplinary exploration.

We create emotional, political, subversive, shared knowledges. These knowledges which fuel our resistances, reflections, creative practices, writings, actions, caring bonds. 

notes: >- alt: >- credit: >- - kind: heading title: >- Second Edition - April 2023 position: 1 level: 2 - kind: block template: gallery title: > position: 2 data: description: >-

In the second edition of Germinar-t we invited the Lab of Rooted Imagination from Barcelona for a 10 day residency to create a performance for the Festival del Agua, an event for local water justice, which the Sunseed Project in Almeria hosts every year.

Outcome of the residency is a play about our (lacking?) connections to territory and linked struggles of positionalities in territories constituted by power dynamics.
