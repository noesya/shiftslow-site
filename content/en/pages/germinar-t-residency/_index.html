Seventeen young humans from Europe and the Americas

A gathering in the Sunseed community in Southeast Spain

Exploring degrowth and art for one week 

Weaving community from degrowth based art practices

Planting seeds of art based degrowth

We activate our imaginations. 

What are our wishes and needs for degrowth based art, for art based degrowth? 

What do we want to manifest?

Walking up and walking down, walking around and around, swimming in the river, dancing in the moonlight, sitting in the fire and around the fire

Knitting, weaving, stitching, painting, collaging, fixing. 

Discussing, debating, singing, humming, clapping, screaming, whispering, being in silence

Making, unmaking. 

Experiment, think, explore, sleep, rest, play, perform, create, celebrate, do, undo.

Degrow. 

We grant ourselves time and space to be together for this week

Blurring the lines that separate us and art and degrowth and community
